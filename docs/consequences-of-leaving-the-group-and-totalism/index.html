Consequences of leaving the group tend to be proportional to the level of totalism

last update: 2020-09-07
(short-analysis)

Mildly Totalistic -> Fully Totalistic

  • Mild Atheism: generally few repercussions
  • Unitarian Universalists: generally few repercussions
  • Traditional protestantism: usually few repercussions
  • Catholicism: may remind you of the penalties you bring upon yourself (damnation, etc)
  • Staunch atheist: some very informal shunning
  • Evangelical Christian: some very informal shunning
  • Mormonism: some may informally shun
  • Modern Islam: some may informally shun
  • JW: formal and informal shunning
  • Scientology: potential Disconnection
  • Fundamentalist evangelical christian: formal shunning
  • Fundamentalist Mormon (older): threat of death to apostates
  • Some newer cults: culminate in end-of-world scenario where all will commit suicide or murder all remaining members (some of these)
  • Fundamentalist Islam: death to apostates
